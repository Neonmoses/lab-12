meet the team

These seven members are the heroes of this universe. Star sheriff was a normal teenager when a meteor hit in the manhattan park and in the meteor was a watch that allow him to use any power to fight bad guys and protect the world. Case heart had a normal life as a smart girl but she lost her love from a criminal that was never cought she use her mind and skills to fight crime and solve mysteries. Dragon man had become a hero when he found a sword that was cast in a stone in the forest cave and when he lifted the sword it gave him the power of a Dragon. Metal spirit had discovered his powers when he was in detention during a lockdown and he uses his powers to fight crime as a spirit. Mighty queen she was a lost queen that had been asleep for centuries and when she awoke her land and her people were all gone but she protects her new home as she did before. Outlaw was a crime fighter when he lost his brother and no longer trusted the law, so he took his own way to fright criminals. Samurai Knight is a warrior she had lost her family from the crime lord where her family had secrets, she was taken in by a wise man who train her and now she fight to protect the people.
